About me

My Story

I graduated from Tennessee Technological University as an Electrical Engineer to follow in the steps of my father, a Nuclear Engineer. It wasn't until years after that I decided that solving problems with Software was my true passion. Driven by that, I became a self-taught Software Engineer majoring in Web Development.

I've always been passionate to learn new things in both tech and leadership. I have strong relational skills and a broad range of experience in web development, leading, teaching, and Agile Web development.
